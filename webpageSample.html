My Project is Called: New Business Finance Help

The purpose of my project is to help people who are wanting to create a start-up business make sure they are financially stable. The process will involve them entering a series of information and the system will calculate it to determine if the business is going to be financially stable, needs more work, more money is need, etc.

The following categories will be addressed:

  • Initial startup cost
  • Saving for an emergency fun
  • Adding projected monthly costs
  • Startup owner's personal budget
  • Estimate potential growth
